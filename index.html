About Our Company

Baba Hatnagar Krishikranti Farmers Producer Company Limited is a registered Private Limited Company incorporated on 25th July 2023 under the Companies Act, 2013. We operate in the Crop and Animal Production sector, specializing in growing vegetables and agricultural commodities.

Located in Rasan, East Midnapore, West Bengal, we are committed to empowering local farmers through collective marketing, quality production, and sustainable agricultural practices. Our mission is to bridge the gap between farmers and consumers while ensuring fair prices for our producer members.
